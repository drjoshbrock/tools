Key Takeaways

  • A triopoly has formed — OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google are effectively tied at the frontier. Choosing between them comes down to workflow fit, not raw capability.
  • The gap is real — Meta and xAI have fallen behind the Big 3 and have not been able to maintain parity.
  • Open-weights is catching up — Chinese labs and open models are within striking distance but still lag by months behind closed frontier models.
  • The frontier is tighter than ever — Five or more models now compete within a few benchmark points of each other.

Key Takeaways

  • The Big 3 dominate — Google, OpenAI, and Anthropic stand well ahead of the pack, with possible signs of recursive self-improvement.
  • xAI has fallen from frontier — Dropped out but promises to return shortly.
  • Meta is re-entering — Not-quite-frontier yet, but a new approach suggests they might be back in the race.
  • Chinese labs are 7-9+ months behind — All six named companies are very much in the race, but lagging released US closed source models.
  • Open weights commitment is slipping — Especially for Xiaomi and Alibaba, raising questions about the future of open-weights AI from China.
  • Mistral has fallen — The sole notable non-US, non-Chinese player has dropped from frontier status.
  • Other US players are far behind — The gap between the Big 3 and everyone else in the US is wide.
